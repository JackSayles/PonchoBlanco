Gear

There is so much stuff you can buy, but not everything makes everyone a better trail runner.

These are the basics: shoes, hydration and clothing. I'm not even going to touch the clothing thing. My rain gear is a $5 white poncho and my shorts are military issue battle dress uniform (BDU) shorts. So to me wear what you want and what you like. Each section is linked to more information.

Shoes- This is really your biggest investment. You can spend lots of money on other 'needs', but the wrong shoe can kill your body and really make it so you end up injured or hate running. So, get the right pair!

Hydation- With hydration there are so many options. Many races require you to have some way to carry a way to say hydrated, this could be even a dixie cup with a lid duck taped to your hand.

Other Things- Treking Poles, micro spikes, water purifiers, watches, GPS devices, etc. You can really get geared out on stuff.
