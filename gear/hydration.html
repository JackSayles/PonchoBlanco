Hydration

On the other end of the spectrum are hydration vests that you can use to carry just about anything you need. My biggest piece of advice here is to try different things out for your self. See if a store will let you return something because it doesn't work for you before you buy it. Think about these questions: Would I mind carrying a water bottle in my hand for a long period of time? Do I like carrying things in my hands or would I rather have my hands free? What might I need to carry with me? Is it small enough to fit into a running pocket? If yes, then do I really need a vest?
Personally I like the vest because I like having my hands free to grab anything.
Once you have made that decision then there is the question of soft flasks or hard bottles?SO really, just try stuff out and see what works best for you. Here is one thing I find so interesting and it is that all of these soft flask bottles and bladders are made the same company for the different brands.
