Other Things

The farther you get into trail running the more you'll be able to fine tune what you will use regularly as well as what you really don't need. I have the poles, micro-spikes, water purifier, and a couple different gps devices and what I use the most is a tracking app on my phone and my vest. The spikes are for running on snow/ice and the purifier is for my long adventure runs when I need to fill up with water again, poles get used on long climbs or really steep and long climbs. So the things I use all the time are my vest to carry hydration, phone and food, everything else is optional and dependent upon the terrain and the length of the run.
